Do this IMMEDIATELY if you believe someone has hacked into your personal email account.

~ Don't panic
~ Log into your email
~ Force logout of all active sessions
~ Change your password
~ Enable 2fa (if necessary)
~ Delete any unknown "forwarding" addresses and rules
~ Delete any unknown mail filters and rules
~ Reset the passwords for all accounts tied to that email address
~ Set up credit monitoring & bank alerts
~ Tell your family, friends, and frequent contacts to be on the lookoutfor scams and fraud coming from "you"
~ Submit a report to the FBI's Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3)
~ Remember to use UNIQUE passwords for each site.
~ If your email password is shared across multiple sites, consider those accounts compromised as well.
